Patching Cydia on old devices

On older devices, most likely from iOS 1.0 to iOS 8.x.x, Cydia gives a "Unable to Load" error. This is because of the Apple update that migrated systems to HTTPS instead of HTTP.

This error can be easily fixed, and will get you in the Cydia homepage in no time!
