Jailbreaking with JailbreakMe Star

  1. Open Safari on the device. This is a WebKit jailbreak so Safari is crucial!
  2. Open jailbreakme.com in Safari.
  3. Slide to the right on "Slide to Jailbreak"

    4. After a few minutes, Cydia should be on the Home Screen.
