How to find your device's iOS Version

Please note some Settings apps may look different, but the steps are the same.

This is a very simple process, and can help you find out if your device is jailbreakable or not.

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Go into the General section.
  3. Go into the About section.

There you should see your iOS Version, device model, and more info.
