Apple JB

Using Carbon

Carbon is an untethered jailbreak for iOS 8.0-9.3.6.

The Carbon menu on an incompatible device.

Optional instructions

  1. Open Safari and go to the Carbon website.
  2. Press "Install Certs".
  3. Press "Install" all the time iOS asks you.

Jailbreak process

  1. Open Safari
  2. Open the Carbon website.
  3. Click on "Run"
  • If the device reboots or crashes unexpectedly not in a jailbroken state, re-run the jailbreak.

To rejailbreak, follow this guide again.

You should now have Cydia on your home screen.
